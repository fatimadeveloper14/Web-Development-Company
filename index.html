Discovery and Business Analysis

The project definition research activities begin with the project discovery phase, in which our business analysts grasp the project's goal and determine the demands of your target audience. It assists them in selecting the appropriate technology stack, defining deliverables, and estimating deadlines and resources.

Front-End Design and Development

Our site designers include user audience demands in UI design after analysing their wants. After finalising the app's appearance and feel with project stakeholders, we bring it to life with front-end development.

Back-End development

Integration

Frequently Asked Question

Why should you create a website for your company?

A well-designed website is critical for your company to attract a larger audience. It attracts web traffic from clients seeking your products and services. Without a doubt, it will improve your company’s revenue and develop your brand image.

How long does it take to create a website?

The time it takes to build a website is determined by several factors, including the development platform, the complexity of the design, the developer’s degree of competence, features and functionality, testing, and so on. Please contact us with your specifications so that we can provide you with an estimated time period.

What process do you follow for web development?

Here are the steps involved in the web development process:
  • Requirement gathering
  • Research and planning
  • Design of site mock-ups
  • Site architecture and database creation
  • Website development
  • Website testing & launching
  • Technical support & maintenance

How much does it cost to develop a website?

The cost of developing a website is totally determined by your business needs. Estimating the actual web development cost is impossible without knowing the in-depth information on numerous variables such as designs, visuals, technology, functionality, integration, and much more.
